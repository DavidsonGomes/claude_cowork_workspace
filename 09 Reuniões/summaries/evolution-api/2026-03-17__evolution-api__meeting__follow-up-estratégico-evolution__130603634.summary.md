# Resumo executivo

## Meeting Purpose

[Define the Evolution product strategy and go-to-market plan.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=8.0)

## Key Takeaways

  - [**Product Strategy:** The "Evolution Cloud" product is deferred. The immediate focus is on monetizing three separate offerings: `Contributor` (early access), `Pro Support` (premium support), and `Academy` (courses).](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=54.0)
  - [**Go-to-Market:** The immediate goal is to generate revenue by the end of April. This is achievable via the `Pro Support` plan, which is projected to quickly acquire 100–300 users at R$99/mo, meeting the R$15k/mo revenue target.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=2026.0)
  - [**Content Workflow:** A new AI-driven workflow will automate content creation (posts, images) from a single topic, enabling rapid, multi-channel distribution.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=1357.0)
  - [**Pricing Model:** `Pro Support` will be monthly-only due to its recurring costs (staff, AI). `Contributor` and `Academy` can offer annual plans to improve cash flow and LTV.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=942.0)

## Topics

### Product & Monetization Strategy

  - [**Evolution Cloud Deferred:** The agency-focused, white-label product is postponed until the core open-source platform is fully validated.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=54.0)
  - [**Immediate Focus → Three Separate Offerings:**](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=195.0)
      - [**1. Contributor (R$89/mo):** Early access to pre-release software and tools.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=595.0)
      - [**2. Pro Support (R$99/mo):** Premium support community with AI triage and dedicated staff.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=616.0)
      - [**3. Academy (R$49/mo intro price):** Video courses and training.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=757.0)
  - [**Rationale for Separation:**](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=564.0)
      - [**Flexibility:** Allows users to purchase only what they need.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=385.0)
      - [**Value Alignment:** `Pro Support` is priced to cover its high recurring costs.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=681.0)
      - [**Strategic Upsells:** Enables clear upgrade paths (e.g., Contributor → Pro Support).](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=403.0)

### Go-to-Market & Content Workflow

  - [**Revenue Goal:** Generate first sales by the end of April.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=1953.0)
      - [**Rationale:** Balance the need for revenue with a partner's (André) caution against rushing the launch.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=1953.0)
  - [**Content Workflow:** An AI agent (Penclaw) will automate content creation.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=1357.0)
      - [**Process:** A single topic → AI generates multi-channel assets (posts, images) → Team approves.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=1357.0)
      - [**First Test:** The team will use "Evo CRM" as the first topic to validate the workflow.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=1718.0)
  - [**Content Cadence:** One central topic per week, distributed across all channels in various formats (posts, videos, lives) to maximize reach.](https://fathom.video/share/goygvP5S9G1k-V2xjCaAxzZ2UhxzsLNc?tab=summary&timestamp=1646.0)
