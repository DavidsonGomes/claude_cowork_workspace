### Financial Reporting Alignment

  - [**Problem:** Discrepancies between cash flow (Davidson) and DRE (Felipe) reports.](https://fathom.video/share/radFXhsByu5WA-tqhg1wHAHJ4SoeQipN?tab=summary&timestamp=1241.0)
      - [**Cause:** DRE uses accrual accounting (revenue recognized when earned), while cash flow uses cash accounting (revenue recognized when received).](https://fathom.video/share/radFXhsByu5WA-tqhg1wHAHJ4SoeQipN?tab=summary&timestamp=1241.0)
      - [**Example:** R$20k in crowdfunding revenue was recognized on the DRE in February but not yet received in the bank account for the cash flow report.](https://fathom.video/share/radFXhsByu5WA-tqhg1wHAHJ4SoeQipN?tab=summary&timestamp=1241.0)
  - [**Solution:** A meeting is scheduled to standardize reporting processes and align on a single source of truth for financial data.](https://fathom.video/share/radFXhsByu5WA-tqhg1wHAHJ4SoeQipN?tab=summary&timestamp=1596.0)
