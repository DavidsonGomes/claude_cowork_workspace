# Resumo executivo

## Meeting Purpose

[Configure Alarcon's sales and onboarding pipelines in Evo.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=254.0)

## Key Takeaways

  - [**Pipeline Strategy:** Adopted a "Funnel" pipeline for sales and separate pipelines for each service's "Onboarding," enabling simultaneous tracking of a single contact across multiple processes.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3331.0)
  - [**Lead Management:** The "Leads Não Fechados" (Lost Leads) pipeline will be simplified to two stages ("Future" vs. "Discarded") to focus resources on viable leads and avoid wasting paid messages on cold contacts.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3158.0)
  - [**Remarketing:** Future remarketing campaigns (e.g., Black Friday) will use dedicated, duplicable pipeline templates to manage specific, time-bound processes.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3448.0)
  - [**Channel Setup Blocked:** WhatsApp/Instagram setup is paused to prevent losing message history, a known risk from a previous attempt. A follow-up is required with Davidson.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=4860.0)

### Pipeline Strategy & Configuration

  - [**Core Principle:** Segment pipelines by distinct business processes (e.g., Sales Funnel vs. Onboarding).](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=414.0)
  - [**"Funil" (Sales Funnel) Pipeline:**](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=2706.0)
      - [**Purpose:** Manage new leads from initial contact to plan presentation.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=2742.0)
      - [**Item Type:** Add a `Contato` (Contact) to track all conversations with one person, or a `Conversa` (Conversation) to track a single interaction.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=1556.0)
  - [**"Onboarding" Pipelines:**](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=2612.0)
      - [**Purpose:** Manage post-sale processes for specific services (e.g., `Consultoria`, `Planilha Personalizada`).](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=2612.0)
      - [**Mechanism:** A single contact can exist in multiple pipelines simultaneously, allowing for parallel tracking.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=1659.0)
  - [**"Leads Não Fechados" (Lost Leads) Pipeline:**](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3019.0)
      - [**Purpose:** Manage leads that did not convert.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3019.0)
      - [**Decision:** Simplify to two stages:](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3158.0)
          - [`Futuro`: Viable leads for future follow-up.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3158.0)
          - [`Descartado`: Non-viable leads to avoid wasting paid messages.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3158.0)
  - [**Remarketing Pipelines:**](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3331.0)
      - [**Purpose:** Manage specific, time-bound campaigns (e.g., Black Friday).](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3367.0)
      - [**Mechanism:** Create a template pipeline and duplicate it for each campaign.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3448.0)

### Pipeline Item Management

  - [**Services & Value:**](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=1860.0)
      - [**Purpose:** Track potential revenue.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=1942.0)
      - [**Mechanism:** Add services (e.g., `Consultoria`) with a value to a pipeline item. The system sums these values per stage and for the entire pipeline.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=1896.0)
      - [**Limitation:** This is a consultative tool, not a financial system. There is no global service list or export function.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3624.0)
  - [**Custom Attributes:**](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=2237.0)
      - [**Purpose:** Add custom data fields to pipeline items.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=2264.0)
      - [**Mechanism:** Create attributes (e.g., `Origem` with values like `Instagram`, `E-book`) in the pipeline settings.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=2303.0)
      - [**Limitation:** Attributes are pipeline-specific and not shared across pipelines.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=3084.0)
  - [**Tasks:**](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=4087.0)
      - [**Purpose:** Create follow-up reminders for specific pipeline items.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=4087.0)
      - [**Limitation:** Tasks do not generate notifications.](https://fathom.video/share/f_7qNPc5inTEPGxqeELaYa-pXSo1sBSm?tab=summary&timestamp=4142.0)
