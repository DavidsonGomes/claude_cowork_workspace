### Strategic Feedback & Revisions

  - [**Positioning:** The current messaging is unclear.](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=718.0)
      - [**Problem:** The brand identity is ambiguous (messaging, marketing, AI, dev?).](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=718.0)
      - [**Solution:** Position as a "Marketing Automation" community.](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=940.0)
          - [**Rationale:** This broader scope allows for future expansion into related tools (e.g., open-source email marketing) under the Evo brand.](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=987.0)
  - [**Pricing & Community Model:** The two-tier plan is insufficient.](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=813.0)
      - [**Problem:** The R$499/mo price is a high barrier for the open-source audience (freelancers, devs).](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=1728.0)
      - [**Solution:** Adopt a three-tier model (Free, Monthly, Annual) to attract a wider audience.](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=813.0)
          - [**Rationale:** A free tier builds a large, engaged base for future monetization. The annual plan should be the primary focus, with pricing designed to cover costs (e.g., support staff, AI tokens) while offering a significant discount.](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=1735.0)
  - [**Content Strategy:** The Academy needs a more structured production process.](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=2170.0)
      - [**Problem:** Relying solely on live streams for course content lacks organization and a clear learning path.](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=2170.0)
      - [**Solution:** Create a structured content pipeline.](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=2170.0)
          - [Produce organized, curated courses on specific topics (e.g., "Cloud Code from Beginner to Advanced").](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=2259.0)
          - [Use multi-streaming (YouTube, Twitch, TikTok) for live "build in public" sessions, then edit the recordings into polished course modules.](https://fathom.video/share/x6zUJet89GrSDhZsT2zPrwNmcDs9-WRv?tab=summary&timestamp=2329.0)
